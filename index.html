Why NY CityPass?

What do I get?

NY CityPass is a booklet of tickets.

There are 5 locations. All those locations will have one of our tour guide. You will meet our guides at appointed time/day.

Our guides will take you around the areas, give 1hr ~ 2hr guide around the area.

After that, you are on your own to explore!! Our guides will give you recommendations for coolest restaurants, sightseeing, bars, you name it!

Along with these 5 tickets for our truly unique and knowledgeble guides, you also get transportation pass!!
