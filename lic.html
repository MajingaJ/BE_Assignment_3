Long Island City

Getting to Long Island City

Long Island City is 5 minute subway ride away from Manhattan!

Take 7 subway from Time Square, after 3 stops, you will get off on Vernon/Jackson stop and you are already in LIC!

When you get out of the subway, our guide will be waiting for you. Make sure to reserve the time/day with our guide at least 24 hours prior! Please email us at blahblah@blahblah.com, or call us at 123-456-7890.

Our guide will give you brief history about the area and will tour the waterfront area. It has the most beautiful view of Manhattan!

After the tour, our guide will give you recommendations of places in Long Island City, and you are on your own to explore.

Tip : From Long Island City, you can take 7 train back to Manhattan. Or you can take East River Ferry to Brooklyn.

East River Ferry could take you to either Williamsburg, Dumbo, or even Midtown Manhattan.

For more info on transportations :

Subways : www.mta.info

Ferry : www.eastriverferry.com
